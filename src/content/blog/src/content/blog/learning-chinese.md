## Why I Started

I began studying Mandarin Chinese independently on October 22, 2024.

My interest began with Chinese culture, communication, and the challenge of learning a language that is different from Korean and English.

## How I Study

My current study routine includes:

- Learning vocabulary
- Practising pronunciation and tones
- Listening to Mandarin content
- Reading short Chinese texts
- Writing simple sentences

## My Goal

My goal is to communicate naturally in Mandarin and use the language during future study, travel, and professional experiences.

## 我的学习方法

我目前的学习内容包括：

- 学习词汇
- 练习发音和声调
- 收听普通话内容
- 阅读简短的中文文章
- 练习写简单的句子

## 我的目标

我的目标是能够自然地使用普通话交流，并在未来的学习、旅行和职业经历中使用中文。
